About the App

In today's world, the climate has become a big issue for human beings; because of an adverse climate change, the weather keeps changing constantly. Too hot or cold weather becomes an obstacle for people to lead their daily routines. Therefore, people must be able to check the weather before leaving their homes. To address people's concerns about the weather, this group created a weather app to show updated weather on time. The weather app enables users to search for weather information based on cities worldwide and returns the necessary weather information for those cities.
